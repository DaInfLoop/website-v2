My "Experience"
Apart from being a developer, I'm also a community moderator, and moderate multiple communities on multiple platforms:

Replit is an AI-powered software development & deployment platform for building, sharing, and shipping software fast.

But before that, Replit was a code-sharing platform where beginners to coding could learn and share. I used to moderate the site from December 2022 to April 2024: catching all sorts of incidents from basic annoyance to platform abuse.

Battle Master Universe is a Discord server I have been and moderated since the dawn of my career.
